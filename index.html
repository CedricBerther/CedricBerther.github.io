I am a Research Assistant at the Department of Cognitive Psychology, Perception and Methodology at the University of Bern, conducted experiments in the field of perception and assisted with studies involving motion platforms, VR headsets, EEGs and caloric vestibular stimulations. I also researched articles for upcoming studies and was involved in preparing the ethics applications.I also worked on other related research projects leading to multiple academic publications. I als processed behavioral data from patients using R.

At the same time I work for my msc in psychology with majors in Clinical Psychology, and Experimental and Applied Cognitive Psychology. I mainly focus on Data Science, Matlab, R, EEG and learning the basics of conducting studies. For my bachelor's thesis I studiet the effectiveness of cognitive behavioral therapy on patients with functional neurological disorders. I graduated my bachelor with a magna cum laude with minors in neuroscience and philosophy.

Before I joined the University of Bern to study psychology, I graduated with an apprenticeship in computer science at the CSS Versicherung. During this time I specialized in application development with a main focus on webpage and app development with Javascript, Java, and PHP.
